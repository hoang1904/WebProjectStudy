Ordering Process

How to Place Your Fashion Order

To place an order, simply browse through our online collection and add your desired items to your shopping cart. Once you've made your selections, proceed to checkout and follow the prompts to complete your purchase.

Timing Is Everything:

For optimal satisfaction, we recommend placing your order with us in advance. While 48 hours is ideal to ensure availability, we understand that custom or large orders may require more time. Feel free to reach out to us as early as possible to secure your preferred items and delivery date.

Delivery and Pickup Options

Do We Offer Delivery?

Absolutely! We offer convenient delivery services within Ho Chi Minh City. Please note that delivery charges may apply based on distance. Simply specify your preferred delivery date and time during checkout, and we'll ensure your order reaches you promptly.

Prefer Pickup?

No problem! We also provide a pickup option at our boutique location. When placing your order, just select the pickup option, and we'll coordinate a convenient pickup time for you.

Payment and Pricing Details

Which Payment Methods Do We Accept?

We offer the convenience of payment via credit card, debit card, and cash on delivery. Rest assured, all transactions are securely processed to safeguard your information.

Are Our Prices Tax-Inclusive?

Yes, our prices are inclusive of all applicable taxes. There are no hidden charges. Absolutely! Our listed prices already include all applicable taxes, ensuring transparency and no surprises at checkout. Shop with confidence knowing there are no hidden charges.

Customization and Special Requests

Can I Customize My Order?

Absolutely! We offer customization options for certain clothing designs. Simply mention your specific requirements in the order notes during checkout, and we'll do our best to bring your vision to life.

Do You Accommodate Dietary Restrictions or Allergies?

We understand the importance of catering to your unique needs. If you have any special dietary restrictions or allergies, please let us know in the order notes, and we'll work with you to find suitable options from our collection.

If you have any other questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're here to help!
