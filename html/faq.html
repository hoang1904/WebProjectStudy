Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering Process

How can I place an order?

You can place an order by visiting our online order form and selecting your desired cake, size, flavors, and any customization options. Once you submit the form, we will contact you to confirm the details and arrange for payment.

How much in advance should I place my order?

We recommend placing your order at least 48 hours in advance to ensure availability. However, for custom or large orders, it's best to contact us as early as possible to secure your desired date.

Delivery and Pickup

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery within Ho Chi Minh city. Delivery charges may apply based on the distance. During the checkout process, you can specify your preferred delivery date and time.

Can I pick up my order?

Certainly! We provide a pickup option at our bakery location. You can select the pickup option during the ordering process, and we'll coordinate the pickup time with you.

Payment and Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payment via credit card, debit card, and cash on delivery. All payments are processed securely.

Are your prices inclusive of taxes?

Yes, our prices are inclusive of all applicable taxes. There are no hidden charges.

Customization and Special Requests

Can I customize the cake design?

Yes, we offer customization options for certain cake designs. Please mention your specific requirements in the order form, and we will do our best to accommodate your request.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions or allergies?

We strive to accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies. Please mention any special dietary requirements or allergies in the order form, and we will advise you on the available options.

If you have any other questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're here to help!
